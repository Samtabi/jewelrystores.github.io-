Is 24K gold pure?

Meanings of the word "gold" are manifold. The term is used to refer to the chemical element itself (its symbol is Au) and also to objects (often jewelry) that contain some gold but do not consist entirely of golda gold watch or ring, for instance. In the latter case, the amount of gold is described by the karat fineness of the alloy, so one sees marks such as 12K for 12 karats, and so forth.
