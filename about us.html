About Us

OUR STORY

In the early 2019, founder Antonina Piatachuk found herself at a jewellery sale in the Ukraine. She is a leader in the fine jewellery industry, with a distinctive collection and a national reputation for excellence. Established in 2019, ANTONINA is a family owned and operated jewellery store located in downtown Kiev’s downtown. ANTONINA is also a diamond and coloured gemstone broker. We are able to offer our clients exceptional service, selection and value. We pride ourselves in carrying only the most beautiful pieces that are guaranteed to become family heirlooms to be passed on and treasured for generations.

We took great care in designing our store. We offer our customers an extraordinary place that combines unique architecture and structure, expressive decor and artistry, and magnificent features all in one great location. ANTONINA is the only jewellery store in Kiev that has a full-service, on-site custom design factory, a jewellery repair and restoration center, gemological lab and watchmakers - all in one location. At ANTONINA, we understand that each customer has different tastes, different needs and an individual style. While our expert staff attend to your every need, you can enjoy a complimentary cup of premium freshly ground coffee from our in-house bar, try on one of the latest masterpieces from Rolex, explore our estate collection, or discover that perfect gift for your special someone. From the thoughtful décor of the store, to the craftsmanship lovingly given to each piece, we guarantee that every visit you make to ANTONINA is a memorable experience.

Aside from our extensive on site services and our own custom designed pieces we showcase many world-renowned international designers in our showroom such as Rolex, Tudor, Hearts on Fire, Fope, Vahan, Tacori, Jack Kelege, Jeff Cooper and Furrer Jacot. As Kiev’s premier fine jeweller, we offer a vast selection of jewellery from classic, contemporary, romantic, vintage and some, a fusion of tradition and modern evolution.
